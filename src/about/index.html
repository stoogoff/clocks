    About Clocks

    Clocks is designed for tabletop roleplaying games, such as Forged in the Dark and Powered by the Apocalypse games, where tracking progress using clocks is a key mechanic. Clocks can have 4, 6, or 8 segments, which can be filled in to show progress towards a goal.

    Use the ADD button at the bottom of the main page to create a clock. You can choose the number of segments, give it a name, and choose a colour. Once the clock is created, clicking anywhere on the clock will fill in a segment of the chosen colour. There’s a close button which deletes the clock, and an eraser button which clears a filled segment.

    Storage

    All clock data is stored within your browser and will be available whenever you visit the site. The advantage of this is there’s no lag when loading your clocks as there’s no network call to retrieve the data. The disadvantage is there’s no way to share clocks with your players. This is a feature I want to add in the future.
